Margins

Margins create space outside an element’s border. Here, each card has a margin to separate it from others.

Padding

Padding is the space inside an element’s border. This card has extra padding to push its content inward.

Borders

Borders wrap around padding and content. Different thickness and colors help visualize the box edge.

Background Colors

Backgrounds make the boundaries of elements visible. Each card here has a distinct background color.
